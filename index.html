About 1of1

About 1of1

1of1 is a one-person studio. That means the person you talk to is the same person who designs your site, writes your code, and answers when something needs fixing.

I've been doing this for 14 years — long enough to know what works, and long enough to know that every business is different. I don't recycle layouts. I don't hand your project off to a junior designer. I build what you actually need.

The goal is simple: a site that looks like you — not like everyone else who used the same builder.
